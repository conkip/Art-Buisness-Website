Help Page

• Navigate pages by clicking on the name in the navigation bar. It stays on the top when you scroll.

• On the homepage you may click to login at the bottom and read the title and website description

• When in the Gallery page, scroll down to see all the artwork along with their names, price, and sizes. You may click on an image of a painting to see a more detailed view.

In this detailed view you may place a bid on a painting by typing into the text field and clicking enter or the bid button. You also may favorite or unfavorite a painting by clicking the heart Icon. Red means favorited.

If you go to your profile, you can see your favorited artwork and the artwork you are currently bidding on.

Finally, the about section has a little blurb about Kasey, and the contact page has her contact information.
