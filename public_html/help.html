Help Page

• On the homepage you may click the button under the title to login and signup. Signing up automatically logs you in and you can logout by clicking the same button again.

• When in the Gallery page, you can click on a painting to see a more detailed view. In the detailed view, you can click the heart icon to like a painting and see it in your profile. You must be logged in to like a painting and see it in your profile.

• If you are interested in a painting you can email or text Kasey with the email/number given in the contact page.
